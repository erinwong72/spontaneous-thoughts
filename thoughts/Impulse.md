# Impulse
$$I = \Delta p= F\Delta t$$
Impulse relates [[momentum]] to force:
$$F = \frac{\Delta{p}}{\Delta{t}}$$

>[!idea]
>Impulse provides a more generalized way of writing Newton’s 2nd law (don’t need constant mass):
>$$F = \frac{mv}{\Delta{t}} = ma$$
